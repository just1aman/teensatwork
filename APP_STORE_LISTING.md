## Description
TeensAtWork connects homeowners with neighborhood teens for everyday household chores — lawn mowing, snow removal, pet care, babysitting, mulching, and more.

FOR HOMEOWNERS:
- Post jobs in seconds with category, description, and hourly rate
- Schedule work for a specific date and time
- Browse teen applicants and read their intro messages
- Pay securely through the app (Stripe)
- Scan a QR code to start and stop a verified work timer
- Every job session is automatically insured
- Cancel free up to 24 hours before the job

FOR TEENS (Ages 13-19):
- Browse available jobs in your neighborhood
- Apply with a personal message
- Get a unique QR code for each job
- Track your hours with a live work timer
- Build real-world skills: responsibility, punctuality, communication
- Earn real money doing real work

SAFETY FIRST:
- Every homeowner undergoes a background check
- Every user is reviewed and approved by an administrator
- All chats are monitored for safety
- Insurance coverage for every active work session
- Parent/guardian info required for teen accounts

HOW IT WORKS:
1. Sign up and get verified by our admin team
2. Homeowners post jobs, teens apply
3. Homeowner selects a teen and pays through the app
4. On job day, teen shows QR code, homeowner scans to start the timer
5. When work is done, scan again to stop — hours and pay calculated automatically

TeensAtWork — where your neighborhood comes together.
