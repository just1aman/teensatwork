Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Introduction

TeensAtWork ("we," "our," or "us") operates the website teens-at-work.com and the TeensAtWork mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform.

Our platform connects homeowners with teen workers (ages 13-19) for neighborhood chores and tasks. Because our platform serves minors, we take privacy and data protection especially seriously.

2. Information We Collect

Account Information
  • All Users: Full name, email address, username, phone number
  • Homeowners: Home address, background check results
  • Teens: Age, parent/guardian name, parent/guardian phone number
  • Google OAuth Users: Google profile ID, name, and email (if you choose to sign in with Google)
Job & Transaction Information
  • Job postings (title, description, category, hourly rate, scheduled times)
  • Job interest/applications
  • Work session data (start time, end time, actual hours worked)
  • Payment information processed through Stripe (we do not store credit card numbers)
  • Insurance certificate records
Communications
  • Chat messages between homeowners and teens
  • These messages are accessible to platform administrators for safety monitoring
Technical Information
  • IP address, browser type, device information
  • Camera access (only when actively scanning QR codes for job check-in/check-out)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To facilitate job posting, matching, and completion
  • To process payments through Stripe
  • To issue insurance certificates for job sessions
  • To verify homeowner backgrounds
  • To monitor platform safety (including chat review by administrators)
  • To communicate with you about your account and jobs
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Our platform allows users aged 13-19 to create accounts. For users under 18:

  • We collect parent/guardian contact information during registration
  • Parent/guardian information is stored and accessible to administrators
  • We do not sell or share teen personal information with third-party advertisers
  • Parents/guardians may request deletion of their child's account by contacting us
  • We collect only the minimum information necessary for the platform to function

5. Information Sharing

We share your information only in these circumstances:

  • Between matched users: When a homeowner accepts a teen for a job, limited contact information is shared to facilitate the work
  • Payment processing: Transaction data is shared with Stripe to process payments
  • Background checks: Homeowner information is shared with our background check provider to verify identity and criminal history
  • Insurance: Job and worker details are shared with our insurance provider to issue coverage certificates
  • Legal requirements: When required by law, subpoena, or to protect safety

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

6. Data Security

  • Passwords are hashed using industry-standard algorithms (pbkdf2:sha256)
  • All data transmitted via HTTPS encryption
  • Payment data handled by Stripe (PCI DSS compliant) — we never see or store card numbers
  • Database access restricted to authorized administrators

7. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Job and payment records are retained for 7 years for legal/tax compliance
  • Chat messages are retained for 2 years
  • You may request account deletion by contacting us

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Opt out of non-essential communications
  • For California residents: additional rights under CCPA

9. Third-Party Services

Our platform integrates with:

  • Google OAuth: For sign-in (governed by Google's Privacy Policy)
  • Stripe: For payment processing (governed by Stripe's Privacy Policy)
  • Background check provider: For homeowner verification

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your rights, contact us at:

  • Email: support@teens-at-work.com
  • Website: https://www.teens-at-work.com
